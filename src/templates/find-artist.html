The AR deepArt Museum embarks on the theme of practical education and comprises an AR museum. As the deadly Covid-19 pandemic swept across the world, it induced a paranormal amount of fear, anxiety and depression in people. But the world also starts to see a surge in the usage of new technology. New ways of education came forward. But with this, the real-life practical experience came to decline. People started feeling boredom seeing 2-D images and text. Educational trips to museums and other destinations came to halt. AR deepArt Museum is a small step to curb this problem as it provides the user with a virtual 3-D museum in their real-time environment containing 3-D models of various artist domains to experience.
